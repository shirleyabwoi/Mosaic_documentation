Welcome to Mosaic

In this guide you'll learn how to use the platform whether you're a trader looking to upcycle unsold clothes, or a designer seelking materials to transform. View demos and step by step guide on how to navigate through our platform.

Onboarding process

The Mosaic application is available for both traders and designers. Our onboarding process is seamless allowing easy navigation for our users from the sign up to the homepgae.

For authentication, our users are required to input their names, email, phone number and passwords of their preference. After agreeing to the terms and conditions, all users are free to acess the application and use it binded by the terms provided.

Trader workflow

  1. Allow locations settings in order to be linked with designers in your area
  2. Browse the designs catalogue
  3. Pick the design you are intrested in and add them to the cart
  4. Confirm order and checkout
  5. Pick a delivery method and drop your unsold clothes there
  6. Track the status of your order
  7. Pay upon delivery of the item

Designer workflow

  1. Allow locations settings in order to be linked with traders in your area
  2. Upload your designs
  3. Check for available designs in the pending listing
  4. Confirm the request
  5. Pick the materials from your preferred pick up point
  6. Redesign the item within 10days and send it back
