The global fashion industry produces 10% of worldwide pollution, with unsustainable textile practices as a leading cause. In Kenya, the Gikomba market is the heart of the second-hand clothing (Mitumba) ecosystem, providing economic opportunity and affordable clothing. However, used and discarded clothing continues to feed landfills like Dandora, increasing the environmental burden.

Our platform addresses this gap by connecting traders with designers for garment upcycling, aiming to turn waste into value and foster sustainable habits in the local fashion market. Through Mosaic, traders are able to renew their unsold clothes while designers use their talent to come up with unique designs. If you are a trader with unsold clothes and designer with talent, Mosaic is the platform for you.

Who is Mosaic for?

Traders

Have unsold clothes taking up space? Mosaic lets you connect with designers who can give your stock a second life. Once redesigned, you get them back and resell tem at better prices.

Here is how to get started in very easy steps:

  1. Sign up to the App
  2. Browse through the designs catalogue and pick out a design of their preference based on the unsold clothes materials that they have
  3. Add the item to the cart
  4. Finish the checkout process by picking the delivery method
  5. Deliver their unsold clothes and wait for delivery
  6. Pay for the new design upon delivery

Designers

Passionate about sustainable fashion? Mosaic gives you a platform to get clients. By uploading your designs you are able to showcase them and get people interested with them.

Here is how to get started in very easy steps:

  1. Sign up
  2. Navigate to the uploads page
  3. Upload your designs by either browsing your gallery or taking a photo
  4. Click upload!

What you need before you start

  • An active email address
  • An active phone number
  • Clear photos of designs(for designers)
