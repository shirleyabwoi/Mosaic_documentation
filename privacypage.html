Introduction

Mosaic is Kenyan based platform, creating solution to sustainability by linking traders to designers. We aim to ensure that our website offers you a nice experience. Please read this privacy policy and provide consent hwere need be for you to have permission to use our services.

Data storage

As a Kenyan based company all our web servers are hosted in Kenya. Our hosting provider adheres to the rules provided by the Kenyan constitution, ensuring that your data is securely stored and GDPR compliant.

Registration data

If you register on our application, we store your credentials and any other information provided at your user profile. You can see, edit or delete your personal information at any time. The information is only accessible to you as a user and our website administrators.

Support data

For the support messages received through our contacts, they are only available to us. These submissions are kept only for customer service purposes and are never used for marketing purposes or shared with third parties.

Cases for using personal data

  1. Verification/ registration of the user
  2. In provision of any techinacl assistance
  3. In customizing our application

Security measures

We use HTTPS protocol throughout our site in order to encrypt the users communications over the servers. In the case of data breach, the system administrators will take immediate action to ensure system integrity. Affected users will be contacted and necessary security measures implemented.

Release of data

In the case of legal purposes, deemed necessary, we shall release ypur information to governement agency. Any passing of personal data will be done with strict adherence to the laws provided by the Kenyan government.
